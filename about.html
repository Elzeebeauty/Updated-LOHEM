ABOUT US

LOHEM's BACKGROUND

With God’s mandate, LOHEM was founded by the President, Evang. Dr. Joy Agbazue, on 29th September 2000. LOHEM is a non-profit making, international, interdenominational Ministry charged with preaching the Gospel to win souls for Christ and impressing the converts with teachings on righteousness that are focused on gaining eternity in Heaven. In her involvement with global evangelization and mission, LOHEM fulfils her mandate by Networking with Pastors, Churches and Mission Agencies. with teachings on righteousness that are focused on gaining eternity in Heaven. OUR VISION

1. Creating a holistic awareness of God's kingdom and populating it.
2. Carrying out mission power-packed outreach crusades in towns and villages.
3. Showing life of righteousness and hence presenting exemplary behavior that imitates Christ.

OUR MISSION

A. To recruit, train and sponsor Missionaries who will preach the Word and finally effect their environment and the whole world with the Gospel.
B. To make genuine converts, disciple them to live in the light of the Word and finally hand them over to Bible believing, practicing Churches around.
C. To network with Pastors and Ministers of the Gospel and influence them to live by the Word that will effect their congregation.

YOUR INVOLVEMENT

Any Christian can be actively involved in this task of populating Heaven through LOHEM Ministry. God’s abundant blessings await you here on earth and in eternity as you do so in many ways listed below:
1. Praying and interceding for LOHEM.
2. Encouraging us through phone calls, letters, E-mails and visitations at our Enugu Office.
3.By your financial help: we need people to sponsor our projects. Eg. The Radio and Television messages as well as crusades/outreaches are very capital intensive and through your sponsorship they can be sustained and gain wider coverage.
4. We are mobilizing Christians who will join us in Personal Evangelism. You can join us today.
5. You can donate Bibles, Christian literature, clothes fairly used dresses and materials, home property for our donation to the underprivileged in the villages.
